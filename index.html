Nigeria

Nigeria is a country in West Africa, bordered by Benin, Niger, Chad, and Cameroon, with a coastline along the Gulf of Guinea. It’s Africa’s most populous nation and has a diverse landscape of plains, rivers, and forests. Nigeria is well known for its abundant natural resources, especially crude oil and natural gas, which are its main exports. It also has significant deposits of coal, tin, limestone, gold, iron ore, and other solid minerals that support industries and local economies.
