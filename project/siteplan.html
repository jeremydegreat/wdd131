Webfingers Advertising Page

Site Name

The site or domain name (along with a logo) is the primary identifier and is critical for branding, visibility, and recognition. Securing a domain name that is available to purchase is normally important but not required in this class. You will need to provide a valid site name relevant to your project, along with a short description of why you chose it.

Proposed Domain Name: webfingersadvertising.com

Description: The name “Webfingers” represents a digital brand that reaches out like fingers through the web — connecting technology, creativity, and innovation. It is memorable, professional, and relevant to IT, cloud, and advertising services.

Site Purpose

The purpose of the Webfingers Advertising Page is to present and promote the company’s IT and advertising services. The website will provide information about our offerings such as Cloud Solutions, Web Development, Mobile App Development, and IT Consulting. It will also serve as a contact and inquiry platform for businesses looking for a reliable technology and digital marketing partner.

Scenarios

  • Scenario 1: “Our company needs reliable Cloud Solutions and Consulting to move our data and operations online securely. We are looking for a trusted IT partner who can handle setup, migration, and management.”
  • Scenario 2: “We want an experienced IT Support and Managed Services provider to maintain our company’s network, monitor systems, and ensure smooth operations across all departments.”
  • Scenario 3: “We are looking for a professional team to deliver Web Development Excellence—creating a responsive, high-performance business website that reflects our brand and helps us attract clients.”
  • Scenario 4: “We need a developer who can create a Mobile App (both native and cross-platform) that connects with our online services and offers a seamless user experience for our customers.”

Color Schema

Primary Color: #1A1A1A (Black) – used for headings, navigation background, and footer areas to give a bold and professional appearance.

Accent Color: #FFD700 (Gold) – used for highlights, call-to-action buttons, and icons to emphasize quality and attract attention.

Color inspiration was taken from Website Builder Expert.

Typography

Primary Font: Poppins – used for headings and titles for a modern, bold look.

Secondary Font: Roboto – used for body text to ensure clarity and readability.

Font choices were selected following recommendations from Google Design.
